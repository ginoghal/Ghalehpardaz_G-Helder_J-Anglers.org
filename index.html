Trout Hatchery

Trout Hatchery

The TRAA first began rearing rainbow trout in an outdoor upwelling incubation box back in 1986. Since then we've grown our facilirtys and successfully taken on both brown trout and brook trout eggs.

Salmonid Monitoring Program

TRAA members and personnel from the Upper Thames River Conservation Authority (UTRCA) waded in for Year 3 of our 5-year Salmonid Tagging and Monitoring Program.
